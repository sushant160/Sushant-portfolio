Hello! I'm Sushant Kumar, an Electronics and Communication Engineering graduate from Netaji Subhas University of Technology with a specialization in IoT. My journey through IoT systems, UI/UX design, and web development introduced me to the power of data—how it explains system behavior, user patterns, and product performance.
